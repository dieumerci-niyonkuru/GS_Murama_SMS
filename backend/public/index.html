About G.S. Murama

Groupe Scolaire Murama is a government-registered school located in Kisaro, Rwanda. We provide education at three levels: Nursery (Early Childhood), Primary (P1-P6), and Secondary (S1-S6). The school serves over 1,000 students from Kisaro and surrounding communities, making it one of the most significant educational institutions in the area.

Our mission is to provide holistic, quality education that prepares students for life, citizenship, and higher learning. We embrace technology and innovation to enhance learning and administration.

Vision: To be a center of academic excellence and digital transformation in Rwanda.

Values: Integrity, Excellence, Discipline, and Community Service.

Academics

Nursery (3–6 years)

Baby Class, Middle Class, Top Class – Play-based learning with focus on social, emotional, and cognitive development.

Primary (P1–P6)

Core subjects: Mathematics, English, Kinyarwanda, Science, Social Studies, French, Creative Arts, and PE.

Secondary (S1–S6)

Advanced curriculum with options in Sciences, Humanities, and Vocational tracks. Preparation for national exams and university entrance.

Digital Learning

All students have access to our online learning portal with course materials, assignments, and progress tracking.
